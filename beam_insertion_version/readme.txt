REQUIRED SOFA PLUGINS:

Sofa version: v21.12
https://github.com/SofaDefrost/STLIB
https://github.com/sofa-framework/BeamAdapter/ 
https://github.com/sofa-framework/SofaPython3

CONTROLS:
(during the simulation, using the numpad)

ctrl+7: increase speed
ctrl+9: decrease speed
ctrl+8: zero speed

ctrl+4: rotate catheter in place in the axis of the tip
ctrl+5: reset catheter rotation
ctrl+6: rotate catheter in the other direction

ctrl+1: move point of insertion in the global positive X axis
ctrl+2: move point of insertion in the global positive Y axis
ctrl+3: move point of insertion in the global positive Z axis

ctrl+M: show all the forces in the next iteration of simulation
ctrl+0: show the controller pose, speed, direction and rotation
